Obtain and Compile qpackman

Use WSL2 to run this command:
qpakman -pic gfx-wad/*.png -o gfx.wad

Then In Windows:
Open WAD file in TexMex and click Edit->New Item->Status Picture

Put in 128x128 and click the constrained box

Click on the picture you just created

Right click and select load picture

Load your personal conchars.bmp

Click File->Save
